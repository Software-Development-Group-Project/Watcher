Features of Watcher

Monitor People

The Watcher system will monitor whether a person is wearing mask or not

Alert the Admin

The system will alert the admin if it detects a person who is not wearing a mask

Alert the Person

If the system identifies a person who is not wearing a mask, it will alert that person to wear a mask

Use Case

University

The Face Mask Detection System can be used at universities to monitor whether people are following the WHO best practices. Our system is best fit for the universities because the management of the universities will already have the data of the students and staffs, so if they do not wear a mask we can easily identify and alert them. The students, academic and non-academic staffs are present within the universities, therfore a security system like Watcher will definitely help the management to monitor a large group of people.

Office

The Face Mask Detection System can be used at offices because it will help to identify employees who are not wearing a mask and alert them. The management will also be notified with the details of the person who was detected by the system for not wearing a mask. Watcher will primarily help to ensure the safety of all the employees because that is the most important thing in this situation.
