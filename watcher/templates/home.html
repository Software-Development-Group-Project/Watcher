Welcome to Watcher


During this difficult times it is best if people try to follow the best practices suggested by WHO to help control the spread of the global pandemic Covid-19, so that everyone can stay safe but there will be some people who will not follow the best practices and that defeats the whole purpose of controlling the spread of Covid-19 pandemic. As a solution for this problem the Watcher system was created! The system will monitor whether a person is wearing a mask or not and it will notify the person if he or she is not wearing a mask.

How watcher works?

Scanning People

Scan people to identify whether all wearing mask or not by scanning their face whether they wear mask or not

Display Video

Displaying live video stream that is scanned by cctv camera. And show people and their detaila if they didn't wear mask

Alert User

If a person detected without mask then watcher will alert them by sending them a message or email and admin
